PROJECTS

Real-Time Facial Emotion Recognition System (FER)

I developed a real-time facial emotion recognition system using the FER2013 dataset, which involved training a model to accurately identify human emotions based on facial expressions. To enhance the model's performance, I experimented with various Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architectures, including VGG19, ResNet50, and Inceptionv3. Through meticulous tuning and optimization, I achieved an impressive accuracy improvement of 82%, significantly optimizing the system's efficiency for real-time emotion detection. This project not only deepened my understanding of deep learning techniques but also showcased my ability to implement complex algorithms in practical applications.

Automatic Detection of Genetic Diseases in Pediatric Age Using Pupillometry

In another project, I developed a system for the early detection of genetic diseases in pediatric patients through non-invasive pupillometry. This innovative approach allowed for the analysis of pupillary responses as a diagnostic tool. By leveraging advanced analytical techniques, I increased the diagnostic accuracy to 75%, which greatly enhances the precision of early medical interventions. This work has the potential to support timely treatments in pediatric healthcare, demonstrating my commitment to applying technology for significant health outcomes.

Identification of the Brachial Plexus Nerve Block Using Machine Learning Techniques

I am currently working on a project that addresses the significant challenge of managing surgical pain, which can be intolerable and agonizing for patients. While general anesthesia is commonly used to mitigate pain, it affects the entire body and can lead to various side effects, including nausea, vomiting, and other complications. Prioritizing patient safety, this project explores alternative methods for pain control, focusing on the identification of the brachial plexus nerve block using machine learning techniques. The goal of this project is to improve the accuracy of identifying the brachial plexus nerve block for regional anesthesia, which numbs only the specific area undergoing surgery or injury. This approach allows patients to remain awake during the procedure, avoiding the need for general anesthesia and significantly reducing the likelihood of systemic side effects. By utilizing machine learning for precise identification, this project aims to enhance pain management techniques, making surgical procedures safer and more focused on patient comfort.

PAPERS

Keyword Extraction System based on Job Description

My research focuses on developing an automated keyword extraction system specifically designed for job descriptions, utilizing advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques. Traditional methods, such as manual identification and basic statistical approaches, often result in inefficiencies and errors, particularly when processing unstructured data. To address these challenges, my research combines supervised methods like Naive Bayes and Support Vector Machines (SVM) with unsupervised techniques, including graph-based algorithms and clustering, to enhance scalability and accuracy across diverse job domains. The system's performance is evaluated using metrics such as precision, recall, and F1-score, ensuring high relevance and coherence in the extracted keywords. Additionally, I have integrated ethical considerations into the design, focusing on data privacy, bias reduction, and fairness. This research aims to improve candidate screening and job matching efficiency, offering a scalable, adaptive solution for the recruitment industry.

Bias and Fairness in Artificial Intelligence

I am currently writing a paper which explores how AI models, despite their increasing use in decision-making across sectors like healthcare, finance, and criminal justice, often exhibit bias, leading to disproportionate outcomes against underrepresented groups. The paper delves into the sources of bias within AI, reviews various approaches to addressing fairness, and discusses future directions for developing more equitable AI systems. It also identifies key technical and ethical challenges involved in creating fair AI, and provides guidelines for incorporating fairness throughout the AI development process to ensure more balanced and just outcomes.
